# Demo 01 — Growth Dashboard Demo Brief

## 1. Demo Purpose

This demo is built as a proof-of-work artifact for Mohamed Yehia's positioning:

**Growth Marketing, E-commerce & AI Transformation Lead**

The goal is to prove the ability to design and build a business-ready growth dashboard that connects marketing, e-commerce, sales performance, customer behavior, and executive reporting.

This dashboard should support the following claims:

- AI-powered dashboards
- Data visualization
- Internal reporting systems
- E-commerce performance analysis
- Marketing performance reporting
- Executive decision support
- Growth operations thinking

---

## 2. Target Audience

The dashboard should impress:

- CEOs
- Founders
- Heads of Growth
- E-commerce Directors
- Marketing Directors
- Digital Transformation Managers
- Recruiters hiring for GCC senior roles
- Business owners looking for practical AI and reporting systems

---

## 3. Business Scenario

Create a realistic sample business scenario inspired by a D2C healthy bakery / FMCG e-commerce brand operating in the UAE.

The dashboard should simulate the business performance of an e-commerce and retail-supported brand across several months.

Suggested business context:

A healthy bakery brand sells online through WooCommerce and also supports offline retail sales through supermarkets and distribution partners. The marketing team runs Meta, Google, TikTok, organic social, email campaigns, and retail activation.

The dashboard helps management understand:

- Sales growth
- Order performance
- Customer acquisition
- Repeat purchase behavior
- Channel performance
- AOV
- ROAS
- CAC
- Conversion trends
- Organic reach
- Paid media efficiency
- Executive summary and recommended actions

### Option B — BI Style Demo

Use:
- Looker Studio
- Google Sheets sample data
- Screenshots embedded into portfolio

Best for:
Recruiters who understand dashboards but do not inspect code.

Build a web dashboard in Next.js and also create a Google Sheets / Looker-style data source explanation.

Recommended:
