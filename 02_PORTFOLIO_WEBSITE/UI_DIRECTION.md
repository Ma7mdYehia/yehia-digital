> Personal profile · digital CV · executive portfolio · case-study library.  
> Positioning: Growth Marketing, E-commerce & AI Transformation Lead.  
> Reference: Stratos template as inspiration only. Proven facts only. HÄLSA rule respected.

## Governing principle

Aim for **premium dark executive**, not futuristic developer portfolio. The UI should feel like the dark-mode investor deck of a consulting, fintech, or serious business systems firm: deep, confident, restrained, and numbers-led.

Glassmorphism is allowed, but dialed down: subtle frosted panels, hairline borders, restrained motion, no neon glow, no animated gradient mesh, no sci-fi AI startup feeling. Where Stratos goes flashy, Yehia.Digital goes quiet and executive.

## Case-study credibility rule

The six-card Selected Work grid is allowed, but only three cards carry hard metrics:

- HÄLSA Bake
- El Shohail Trading
- IDE / IDEAEG Education Group

The other cards can carry sector, market, role, and qualitative descriptions only. Do not invent numbers.

## 2. UI notes per section

### Left floating icon nav

Fixed vertical rail on desktop: home, work, about, experience, contact, and CV/download. Icon-only with tooltip labels on hover. Active state uses sage green. On mobile it collapses into a top hamburger menu.

### Hero

Full-height first screen. Left side: name, positioning, value statement, two CTAs. Right side or background: large hero visual. Preferred: professional headshot inside a rounded frosted frame. If no headshot is ready, use a restrained abstract dark placeholder.

### Stats / proof strip

3–4 key metrics with large numbers and small source attribution. Count-up animation once on scroll into view.

### About snapshot

Short paragraph and a “Read my story” link. Optional credential card echoing Stratos profile-card style, but without gamification.

### What I do

Three glass cards: Growth & Performance, E-commerce & Business Systems, AI-Augmented Operations.

### Tools Behind My Work

Horizontal draggable carousel of dark rounded cards. Each card includes tool icon, tool name, and one-line business use case. Present as operating stack, not developer tech stack.

### Selected Work

Filter menu above a large visual grid. Two columns on desktop, one column on mobile. Full-bleed image cards with bottom glass overlay, project title, tags, and optional metric chip only for verified metric-backed cards.

### Professional Journey

Vertical timeline, oldest to newest, showing the professional arc from 2005 digital builder to current group marketing and digital growth role.

### How I Work

Editorial block, not flashy grid. Explains the AI-augmented operating model, GitHub-tracked workflow, Claude/ChatGPT, ClickUp, dashboards, and systems thinking as working style.

### Contact CTA

Strong final block with email, LinkedIn, and Download CV.

## 5. Final visual system

### Palette

- Base background: `#0B1220`
- Secondary surface: `#0F1724`
- Glass panels: white at 4–8% alpha over base
- Border: white at 8–12% alpha
- Primary accent: sage green `#3DBA8C`
- Optional secondary accent: muted amber `#E0A458`
- Primary text: `#E8EDF2`
- Secondary text: `#94A3B8`

### Typography

Use one clean modern sans such as Inter, General Sans, or Geist. Use regular and medium weights. Large hero name, confident section titles, large stat numbers, readable body text.

### Card style

Rounded 16px, frosted glass, subtle border, generous padding. Hover: slightly brighter border and subtle lift or image zoom. No heavy shadow.

### Motion

Fade-and-rise on scroll, 400ms ease-out. Staggered lists. Stats count-up once. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

### Spacing

Generous section rhythm, strong grid, calm whitespace.

### Mobile behavior

Left rail becomes top hamburger. Hero stacks. Stats 2-up then 1-up. Cards single column. Tools carousel horizontal scroll.
