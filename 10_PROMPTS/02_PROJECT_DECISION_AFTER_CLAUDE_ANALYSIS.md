# Project Decision After Claude CV Strategy Analysis

## 1. Strategic Decision

Claude's analysis confirmed that the new positioning is strong but must be supported by proof-of-work demos before active job applications.

The final target positioning remains:

**Growth Marketing, E-commerce & AI Transformation Lead**

However, the project will be built in two layers:

### Internal Build Positioning

Growth Marketing, E-commerce & AI Transformation Lead

This is the final positioning we are building toward.

### Public Safety Positioning

Senior Growth & E-commerce Leader | AI-Augmented Marketing Operations

This can be used only if a CV is needed before the proof-of-work demos are complete.

---

## 2. Final Direction

We will not rush the final job-application CV before the demos are ready.

Instead, we will build the proof layer first, then finalize the CV, portfolio website, premium PDF, and LinkedIn profile.

---

## 3. Proof-of-Work Priority

The demos will be built in this order:

### Demo 01 — Growth Dashboard Demo

Purpose:
Prove AI-powered dashboards, reporting, data visualization, marketing analytics, and e-commerce performance understanding.

Suggested features:
- Revenue overview
- Orders
- AOV
- Repeat rate
- Channel performance
- CAC / ROAS simulation
- Organic reach
- Paid media performance
- Executive summary
- Sample data
- Dashboard screenshots

### Demo 02 — AI Social Automation Demo

Purpose:
Prove AI social automation workflows for content planning, creative generation, publishing structure, and performance reporting.

Suggested workflow:
- Content brief input
- AI caption generation
- Creative direction generation
- Channel adaptation
- Publishing matrix
- Performance log
- Next-cycle optimization prompts

### Demo 03 — Company Reporting System Demo

Purpose:
Prove internal reporting workflows for management decision-making.

Suggested features:
- Weekly performance report
- Sales summary
- E-commerce summary
- Marketing summary
- Distribution / product movement mock data
- AI-assisted executive summary
- Exportable PDF/report format

### Demo 04 — Portfolio Website

Purpose:
Make yehia.digital itself a proof-of-work artifact.

The site should include:
- Home

- AI Lab
- Dashboards
- About
